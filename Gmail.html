Download Gmail App for Android and Ios

-Open Google Play Store on your Android phone or tablet.

-Search for Gmail in the app store or Play store.

-Tap the Install button to instantly download and install the Gmail app on your device.

How to Sign Up for Gmail

Step 1
Go to gmail.com and hit the Create an account button.

Step 2
Complete your first name and last name, then click Next.

Step 3
Enter your birthday and gender, then click Next.

Step 4
Choose a Gmail address or create your own, then click Next.

Step 5
Create a password for your Gmail account.

Step 6
Recovery email details for added security

Step 7
Save your Gmail account in your browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Brave).

Step 8
Your Gmail account setup is complete.
