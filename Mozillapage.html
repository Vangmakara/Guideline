How to install and setup Mozilla Thunderbird

រៀនតម្លើង Mozilla Thunderbird តាមដំណាក់កាល

Follow our detailed guide to configure Thunderbird for your email needs.

Installation Steps

Step 1
Visit this Thunderbird download page in any browser, such as Microsoft Edge.

Step 2
Click the button. The Thunderbird Installer will automatically offer you the best available version for your computer.

Step 3
Click Open file in the Downloads notification on Microsoft Edge to start the process.

Step4
Click Choose Standard and click Next.

Step 5
Then click Install this program.

Step 6
Click Finish.

Step 7
When in Thunderbird, set up your existing email address with your username, email, and password.

Step 8
Then click the Set Default button. Your installation and setup are finished.
